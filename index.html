Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

How do I know which plan is right for me?

If you need ongoing analytics support, go for our Monthly Retainer. If you need a quick fix or occasional help, our On-Demand (Day Rate) model is ideal. If you need a focused, high-impact project, choose our Deep Dive engagement. 📅 Still unsure? [Book a Free Consultation](/consultation) and we’ll help you decide.

Can we customize a Monthly Retainer plan?

Absolutely! Our retainers are tailored to your needs, with fixed monthly hours and a clear scope based on your business priorities.

What does a Deep Dive project include?

It depends on your needs! It could be event tracking setup, dashboard builds, data cleanup, or advanced analytics work. We’ll define the scope together before kicking off.

Do you offer training or team handover?

Yes! For project-based work, we provide documentation and walkthroughs so your team can manage analytics confidently after we deliver.

What analytics tools do you work with?

We support Mixpanel, Heap, Amplitude, Segment, Looker, Power BI, Google Analytics 4, Optimizely, LaunchDarkly, and more.

What industries do you specialize in?

We primarily work with SaaS, E-commerce, Fintech, B2B, and Digital Marketplaces, but we’re happy to discuss your specific needs.
